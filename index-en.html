  • PPF - Paint Protection Film

    Preserve the beauty of your car with our innovative paint protection. We utilize modern technology to apply an invisible, protective film that effectively shields against rock chips, insects, and harmful UV rays.

    Paint Protection Film (PPF) is a polyurethane material that constitutes the most durable type of paint protection available on the market. This solution is characterized by extremely long durability, lasting up to 10 years (with a 5-year installation guarantee). Protecting a car with PPF is especially dedicated to those who have invested significantly in new cars and wish to safeguard the vehicle against significant depreciation.

    We offer the latest series from renowned brands like 3M and STEK, which have self-healing properties, are anti-yellowing, and have a thickness of 200 microns (compared to the original paint layers which typically have between 70-100 microns). PPF film provides complete protection against rock chip damage and scratches with its self-healing technology and can be applied to a variety of surfaces, including cars, boats, trucks, car interiors, and more. The most popular and recognized PPF packages include:

    • Most Exposed Areas
    • Full Front
    • Full Car Wrapping Clear (gloss)
    • Color Change Matte (from gloss to matte)
    • Custom tasks, including Carbon PPF, Color PPF, and High Gloss Black PPF, ideal for the current two-tone paint trend.

  • Ceramic Coating

    Protect your investment with our advanced ceramic paint coating. This long-lasting solution provides an invisible barrier against dirt, pollutants, and UV rays, while giving your vehicle a brilliant shine.

    Thanks to our outstanding market products, continuously updated and recognized worldwide for their durable properties, as well as our best-selling product line with a duration of 36 months/40,000 km, we can undoubtedly stand behind the quality of our services.

    The ceramic coating contributes to protecting the paint in several ways. Firstly, it has hydrophobic properties that repel water from the bodywork, allowing it to bead and roll off. This feature also makes it harder for pollutants to adhere to the surface. The coating also enhances the depth of the paint, giving the car a brilliant and glassy appearance. Furthermore, the ceramic coating makes the paint more resistant to UV radiation, preventing fading over time.

    The quality of the ceramic coating and the application process play a crucial role in the outcome. Our products are internationally renowned for their gloss and hydrophobic properties, and our ceramic coating is classified with a 9H hardness. This coating is applied by experienced and certified professionals, and the best results are achieved through proper preparation of the car's paint and multiple steps of polishing and paint correction.

  • Paint Correction

    Revive the shine of your vehicle's surface with our professional polishing service. Remove scratches, oxidation, and imperfections, and achieve a mirror-like finish that makes your car shine like new.

    Paint correction is a process that selectively removes swirl marks, scratches, and other defects from the car's paint. The aim of this treatment is to eliminate imperfections and restore the paint to its original condition. With us, as well as with other professional specialists, three types of paint correction are offered: One-Step, 2-step, and 3-Step. The latter, known as full paint correction, is used on the most demanding surfaces. It allows us to remove up to approximately 90% of scratches, and after the treatment, the car appears highly presentable under all lighting and weather conditions.

    The choice of the right method should be left to the specialists treating the vehicle. We professionally consider each car and conduct a thorough inspection of the condition and thickness of the paint before starting the polishing.

    After such treatment, aiming to achieve maximum and best possible results while protecting the car in the long run, we would gladly recommend considering our paint protection service for further security and maintenance.

    Enhance your truck's paint with our advanced ceramic paint coating. Resist dirt, road salt, harmful UV rays, and weather impact while maintaining a brilliant and reflective surface.
